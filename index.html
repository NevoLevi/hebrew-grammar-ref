Focus of this volume

The through-line is the future tense (עתיד) built out binyan-by-binyan, plus the imperative and the whole Nif'al system.

01

The Verb System · הַפּוֹעַל

The heart of the book: each binyan is carried into the future, across sound roots and the weak root-types (גְּזָרוֹת). Then the imperative and the passive participle, and finally the Nif'al in all three tenses.
